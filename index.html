Greenville might be full of southern charm, but it’s also got something else—challenging weather. Intense rain, high humidity, and the yearly threat of hurricane season can turn a poorly installed or repaired roof into a nightmare for homeowners.

Think about it. Those summer thunderstorms can sneak through leaky spots, turning your cozy attic into a moldy mess. Winter cold snaps? They can cause cracks in low-quality roofing materials, creating an open invitation for water damage. And hurricanes? You don’t want to find out the hard way how strong (or weak) your roof is during gale-force winds.
